About Me

Hello! My name is Tiifany, and I was born and raised in the beautiful state of Arizona. Growing up in the desert, it might seem surprising, but one of my greatest passions is being on or near the water. There's something incredibly serene and rejuvenating about the sound of waves crashing against the shore or the gentle ripple of a tranquil lake. When I'm not daydreaming about oceanic adventures, you can often find me jamming out to the tunes of Taylor Swift. Her music has a way of capturing emotions and experiences in a relatable and heartfelt manner that speaks to my soul. Another one of my cherished pastimes is visiting Disneyland. The magical atmosphere, exhilarating rides, and enchanting characters make me feel like a kid again. Lastly, I have an insatiable wanderlust. In 2020, I embarked on an unforgettable journey to Russia, where I volunteered as an English teacher. The experience was eye-opening, allowing me to immerse myself in a new culture while also helping others. Additionally, I've had the opportunity to travel within the United States, working in breathtaking places such as Alaska, Yellowstone, and Idaho, where nature's splendor never fails to leave me in awe.

Driven by my desire for personal and professional growth, I recently made a significant decision to join the ASU Coding Bootcamp, aiming to learn a new skill and pursue a career change. The world of coding has always fascinated me, with its limitless potential to create, innovate, and solve problems. Eager to delve into this exciting realm, I enrolled in the bootcamp to gain comprehensive knowledge in programming languages, web development, and software engineering. The rigorous curriculum and hands-on projects have challenged me, pushing the boundaries of my problem-solving abilities while fostering collaboration within a dynamic learning environment. Through this immersive experience, I am equipping myself with the necessary tools and skills to thrive in the ever-evolving field of technology. With a newfound passion for coding and a determination to make a meaningful impact, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead as I embark on this transformative journey towards a fulfilling career in the world of tech.
